🌋 Mission Objective

Following Mt. Pinatubo's historic eruption, massive volcanic ash deposits remain resting on the high slopes.

Your Task: Heavy monsoon rains convert this loose ash into destructive mudflows called lahar. As the disaster response engineer, strategically deploy engineering tools along active channels to slow, absorb, or divert flows before they bury vital down-river communities.
